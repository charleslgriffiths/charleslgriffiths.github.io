SenseFit (COMP6013)

Developing SenseFit ‚Äì a machine learning-powered mobile application designed to predict injury risks for amateur and semi-professional athletes. Unlike existing solutions tailored for elite sports, SenseFit focuses on accessibility and usability. The app analyzes key biometric data such as heart rate variability (HRV), sleep quality, and fatigue levels to provide personalized injury risk assessments and proactive training recommendations. It integrates advanced ML models like Random Forest, k-Nearest Neighbors (kNN), and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks to detect trends and optimize injury prevention strategies.
